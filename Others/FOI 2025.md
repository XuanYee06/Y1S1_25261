# 🎡 Festival of Ideas (FOI) 2025 — 4-Day Event Experience

### 📅 Event Details  
**Date:** 4 – 7 November 2025  
**Venue:** Putrajaya International Convention Centre (PICC)  
**Organised by:** Ministry of Higher Education (MOHE), Malaysia  

---

### 🎯 About FOI 2025  
Festival of Ideas (FOI) 2025 is an annual event that celebrates innovation, creativity, and collaboration among universities, industries and communities. It held at the Putrajaya International Convention Centre (PICC), the event featured forums, exhibitions, workshops and showcases from local and international participants. The goal of FOI is to promote innovative thinking and knowledge sharing to empower youth and students to bring their ideas to life and contribute to Malaysia’s progress.

---

### 🧍‍♀️ My Role  
I attended FOI 2025 as a student from Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM). Over four days, I explored the exhibition halls, participated in workshops, attended talks and experienced the creative atmosphere of the event in PJ.

---

### 🗓️ My 4-Day Journey  

#### 🏛️ **Day 1 – Opening Ceremony & Exploration**  
- Attended the official opening ceremony, marking the beginning of the festival.  
- Explored various innovation booths and exhibitions including:
  - Research & Innovation Exhibition  
  - HEIs@FOI Pavilion  
  - TVET Showcase  
  - Industry Showcase  
- Learned about projects from universities and industries focusing on AI, sustainability, digital transformation and entrepreneurship.

---

#### 🍰 **Day 2 – Hands-On Workshops**  
- Joined interactive workshops where I decorate small cupcakes and learned popcorn packaging.  
- This activity taught me the importance of creativity and attention to detail.  
- It was a fun and relaxing way to experience innovation through practical learning.

#### 🧠 **Day 3 – Talks and Forums**  
- Attended insightful talks by educators and industry experts.  
- Some of the sessions included:
  - University–Industry Partnerships in Driving Digital Economic Growth & AI Evolution  
  - From Skies to Space: Driving Malaysia’s Aerospace Growth through Innovation & Talent  
  - Navigating a New World forum  
  - AI Dreams and Our Creative Future Together  
- These sessions broadened my understanding of how technology, research and creativity shape Malaysia’s future.

---

#### 🌟 **Day 4 – Closing Ceremony**  
- The highlight of FOI 2025 was the **attendance of Malaysia’s Prime Minister, Dato’ Seri Anwar Ibrahim**.  
- He delivered an inspiring speech on youth innovation and the MADANI vision, encouraging students to think creatively and contribute to the nation’s growth.  
- The event concluded with awards for outstanding projects and a message of empowerment for young innovators.

During the four days, I gained valuable insights such as:
