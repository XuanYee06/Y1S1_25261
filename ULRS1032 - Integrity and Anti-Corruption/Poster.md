## 🧠 Reflection
Before starting to create a poster, my group members and I held some physical and online meetings to choose a relevant issue and topic related to the task requirement. In addition, we conduct some background research to understand the problem better. During our meetings, we identified the objectives, created survey questions and planned how to collect data. Moreover, we divide responsibilities equally and each member played a role in gathering information, analysing responses and writing the content after we have selected the poster template in Canva.

We summarized and designed the important parts such as methodology and findings into a poster. It took us some time to revise the layout, check the accuracy of facts and ensure a proper referencing. After submitting this poster, we are required to present our poster and ideas to our friends in front of the lecture hall. In my opinion, although this task was difficult, it has improved my research skills and presentation abilities. 

All in all, the discussions and collaboration helped us to complete the task successfully and reinforced my understanding about the crucial role played by integrity in combating corruption in the society.
