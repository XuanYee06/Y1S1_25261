# Integrity Week Talk
📆Date: 10 December 2025  
📍Location: Lecture Hall 7, Block N24, Universiti Teknologi Malaysia   
🕜Time: 2pm - 5pm   
<br>

## 🧠 Reflection
Throughout the talk, I gained a deeper understanding of integrity, accountability and anti-corruption strategies after listening to experienced alumni and SPRM Johor officers. In my opinion, the interactive Q&A sessions encouraged thoughtful discussions. It is inspiring for me to see activities to promote integrity being conducted in the university community. It reminds me of the moral of integrity in life.

All in all, this event reminded me the importance of understanding and practicing ethical values and maintaining integrity throughout my time at UTM and beyond.
