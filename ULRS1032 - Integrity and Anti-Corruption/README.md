# Integrity and Anti-Corruption
👨‍🏫**Lecturer:** Dr. Fahrul Irfan bin Ishak  
🏫**Section:** 14  

This course focuses on ethics, honesty, and values in personal and professional life. It also introduces Malaysia’s efforts in promoting integrity and combating corruption. We will learn how integrity is essential not only for leadership but also for building trust in organizations and society.

## 📘 Topics Covered
- Principles of integrity and moral values  
- Effects of corruption on society  
- Anti-corruption laws and practices  
- Case studies and real-life examples  
<br>

## 🧠 Reflection
In this course, I learned about the importance of ethics, honesty, and integrity in both personal and professional life. I discovered how corruption affects society, the economy, and organizational trust. Through case studies and discussions, I understood Malaysia’s initiatives and laws in preventing corruption. This course taught me that success is not only about knowledge or skills, but also about maintaining strong moral values and ethical principles in every action I take.
