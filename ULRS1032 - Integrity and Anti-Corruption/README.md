# ULRS1032 - Integrity and Anti-Corruption
👨‍🏫**Lecturer:** Dr. Fahrul Irfan bin Ishak  
🏫**Section:** 14  

This course focuses on ethics, honesty and values. It also introduces Malaysia’s efforts in promoting integrity and combating corruption. We will learn why integrity is not only important for leaders but also in an organizational and societal context.  
<br>

![moral](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c8c6cf4d-4b65-4fcc-a991-20a31cb89bbf)


## 📘 Topics Covered
*Topik 1:* Konsep dan Kepentingan Nilai Dalam Kehidupan  
*Topik 2:* Nilai Dalaman Dengan Pembentangan Jati Diri Dalam Menangani Rasuah  
*Topik 3:* Integriti Teras Kesejahteraan  
*Topik 4:* Rasuah Suatu Jenayah Serius  
*Topik 5:* Agensi Pencegahan Rasuah dan Pemerkasaan Integriti  
*Topik 6:* Rasuah Berdasarkan Akta SPRM 2009  
*Topik 7:* Liabiliti Korporat  
*Topik 8:* Jenayah Kolar Putih  
*Topik 9:* Punca, Simptom dan Kesan Rasuah  
*Topik 10:* Laporkan Rasuah, Langkah Pencegahan dan Inisiatif Kerajaan  
*Topik 11:* Institusi Sosial Dalam Pembudayaan Integriti dan Anti-Rasuah

## 🧠 Reflection
In this course, I realized that ethics and integrity are really important in my personal life and my professional life. I was able to learn the effects of corruption in our society and economy. Through case studies and discussions, I understood the efforts of Malaysia in addressing the problem of corruption. In my opinion, I think that using real life examples and acting out scenarios like the video we acted would make learning about integrity more interesting and it would really help people understand the importance of integrity and ethics. This course taught me that achieving success does not only focus on knowledge or skills, but also about maintaining strong moral values and ethical principles in every action I take.
