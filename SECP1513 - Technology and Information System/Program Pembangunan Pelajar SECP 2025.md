# 💼 Program Pembangunan Pelajar SECP 2025

📅 Date: 8 November 2025 (Saturday)  
🕔 Time: 8:45am - 5:00pm  
📍 Venue: Dewan Seminar Kejora & N28 Lecture Rooms  

---

## ✨ Summary
The Program Pembangunan Pelajar SECP 2025 was organized to strengthen students’ professional development and readiness for the workplace. The program focused on several key aspects, such as UTM Graduate Attributes, effective CV writing, experience sharing by Year 4 seniors and group-based activities to enhance our teamwork and leadership skills.  

Two invited speakers were En. Baharudin Mastari, Timbalan Penolong Kanan of faculty of Computing and Ms. Noor Hazwani Ramli, Executive at PETRONAS (2023–present) and founder of GetMeHired. Both speakers shared valuable insights on professional growth, employability and experiences. 
<br>

---

## 🧠 Reflection
Through this program, I learned the importance of aligning personal goals with UTM’s Graduate Attributes, such as communication, leadership and lifelong learning. The CV writing session was particularly useful because the speaker taught me a lots of practical tips to present my qualifications and experiences more effectively. Furthermore, the senior sharing sessions and group activities helped me understand the importance of teamwork and adaptability in professional settings.  

During the group activities, my senior shared some valuable experiences for us such as we are encourage to participate on each of the competitions especially Hackathon to gain our experience. Overall, this program enhanced my awareness of career preparation and inspired me to continuously develop both academics and soft skills to succeed in my future career.
