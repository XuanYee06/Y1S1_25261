# 💼 Program Pembangunan Pelajar SECP 2025

📅 Date: 8 November 2025 (Saturday)  
🕔 Time: 8:45am - 5:00pm  
📍 Venue: Dewan Seminar Kejora & N28 Lecture Rooms  

## ✨ Summary
The Program Pembangunan Pelajar SECP 2025 was organized to strengthen students’ professional development and readiness for the workplace. The program focused on several key aspects, including UTM Graduate Attributes, effective CV writing, experience sharing by final-year students, and group-based activities designed to enhance teamwork and leadership skills.  
Two invited speakers were featured: En. Baharudin Mastari, Deputy Registrar of the Faculty of Computing, and Ms. Noor Hazwani Ramli, Executive at PETRONAS (2023–present) and founder of GetMeHired. Both speakers shared valuable insights on professional growth, employability, and personal branding.  

## 🧠 Reflection
Through this program, I learned the importance of aligning personal goals with UTM’s Graduate Attributes, such as communication, leadership, and lifelong learning. The CV writing session was particularly useful, providing practical tips to present my qualifications and experiences more effectively. The sharing sessions and group activities helped me understand the significance of teamwork and adaptability in professional settings. Overall, this program enhanced my awareness of career preparation and inspired me to continuously develop both technical and soft skills to succeed in my future career.
