# 💼 Program Pembangunan Pelajar SECP 2025

📅 Date: 8 November 2025 (Saturday)  
🕔 Time: 8:45am - 5:00pm  
📍 Venue: Dewan Seminar Kejora & N28 Lecture Rooms  

---

## ✨ Summary
The Program Pembangunan Pelajar SECP 2025 was organized to enchance students’ professional development and preparedness in the working field. The program focused on several key aspects, which include the UTM Graduate Attributes, how to write an effective CV, sharing of experiences by Year 4 seniors and group activities to enhance our teamwork and leadership skills.  

There were two invited speakers and they included En. Baharudin Mastari, Timbalan Penolong Kanan of the faculty of Computing and Ms. Noor Hazwani Ramli, Executive of PETRONAS (2023–present) and the founder of GetMeHired. All speakers shared valuable insights on professional growth, employability and experiences. 
<br>

---

## 🧠 Reflection
Throughout this program, I learned the importance of integrating my personal goals with UTM’s Graduate Attributes, such as communication skills, leadership and lifelong learning. The presentation session on writing a CV was very useful because the speaker provided valuable tips that taught me a lot about how I can make my CV more attractive. Furthermore, the senior sharing sessions helped me to understand the importance of teamwork and adaptability in professional settings.  

During the group activities, my senior shared some valuable experiences for us such as we are encourage to participate in every competition especially in the Hackathon to gain my experience. Overall, this program has helped to increase my awareness in relation to my future career preparations.
