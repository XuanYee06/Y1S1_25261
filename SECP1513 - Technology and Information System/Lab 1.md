## 🧠 Reflection
During the lab session, we learned how to assemble and disassemble a computer. It was the first time for me to disassemble a computer and see its internal computer hardware. The lecturer showed us the correct ways to handle the computer components such as the motherboard, RAM, power supply and hard disk. After the demonstration, we practiced how to assemble and disassemble the PC in a group of six.

Though this lab session, I have the opportunity to learn how each component connects and functions together to form a complete system. It was exciting to experience hands-on work instead of only learning from slides. Doing the lab in a group also helped to improve my teamwork, patience and communication skills when sharing tools and completing tasks together. Thanks to Dr. Aryati for giving us this opportunity to gain my experience.
