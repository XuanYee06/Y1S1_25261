## 🧠 Reflection
At the beginning of the talk, the background of PPG was introduced by the speaker. It was learned from a previous industry talk that PPG is a chemical company involved in the production and distribution of paints and coatings, as well as the supply of silica products for tyres and rubber. Power BI was described essentially as a reporting tool. Furthermore, real-life industry processes were illustrated through a dessert-making analogy to demonstrate how information systems are operated in practice.

The relationship between the culinary process and data systems is illustrated in **Figure 1.0**, while the specific technical mappings are detailed in **Table 1.0**.

In this analogy, data such as videos, audio, and images is represented by dessert ingredients. At PPG, sales and customer data are processed on a daily basis. Just as ingredients must be obtained from specific sources, data is also derived from various origins. Data sources, such as SQL Server and SAP, are represented by ingredient suppliers. The transfer of these ingredients to a restaurant represents logistics, which was compared to networks like the Internet, VPN, and LAN. These networks are utilized to determine the most efficient routes for data transmission.

Once the ingredients are received, they are stored in a freezer, representing storage systems such as local databases and data warehouses. Hardware, including servers and cloud computing, is represented by baking appliances. The logic and documentation of the data, handled by software like Power BI, are symbolized by a recipe book. The role of the chef is performed by the BI developer, by whom reports and dashboards are built and raw data is transformed into tables and charts. Finally, the business users who request and view these reports are represented by the customers.

It was also mentioned by the speaker that Power BI is not merely a software application, but a platform through which connections to many different data sources can be established. Three main components are included:
> - Power BI Desktop
> - Power BI Service
> - Power BI Mobile  
<br>

An incomplete system architecture was demonstrated to explain how data is flowed into Power BI and how VPNs can be modified to ensure that the correct information is viewed by the appropriate individuals.
