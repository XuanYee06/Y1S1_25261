## 📌 About the Talk
This industry session was conducted by PPG, a global leader in coatings, paints and specialty materials. The talk covered:
- Introduction to PPG and its global presence
- Data Analytics
- Global Infrastructure
- SAP Overview  
- Internship and employment opportunities for students  
<br>

## 🧠 Reflection
A detailed presentation of PPG Industries in the global market was provided during the recent talk. As a major supplier of paints and coatings, the company plays a key role in protecting products like electronic screens and industrial packaging. The opening session showed that PPG is more than a manufacturer, it is a large corporation focused on technological growth.

In the data analysis section, it was noted that skills in programming, data types, and visualization are essential. However, it was clearly stated that the most important technical skills are SQL and database design. It was also understood that successful data work requires more than technical ability, it depends on strong logic and problem-solving skills.

The talk on global infrastructure explained the main systems that support a large company, such as cloud technology, networking, and cybersecurity. Following this, the SAP session showed how Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) helps manage internal work and supply chain activities. These sections combined to show that technology roles are not separate from each other. Instead, they are all connected, and understanding how these different systems work together is necessary for a global business to run smoothly.

On a larger scale, it was reinforced through the presentation that career preparation necessitates more than the mere completion of a degree. A balance between academic study and hands-on experience is required. This point was specifically highlighted during the Q&A session when a question was raised regarding whether a candidate with perfect grades but limited projects would be preferred over one with average grades and a strong GitHub portfolio. In response, it was explained by PPG that they look at the whole person rather than just their grades. Much value is placed on project experience, outside activities, and how a person communicates during an interview. A significant emphasis was placed on the necessity of applying academic concepts to resolve actual industrial challenges. It was ultimately determined that although high grades establish a vital baseline of knowledge, the primary objective is the successful transition from theoretical understanding to the implementation of practical solutions.  
<br>

## 🤪 Thoughts & Impressions
After this sharing session, I show increasing interest in the PPG industry. PPG has always been one of my ideal internship companies because I found that the company has a full set of technologies. In my opinion, I believe that the profession still has room for developments that suit more companies. Hence, learning more in the profession will bring me a better prospect.

Apart from technology, I realized that the company focuses on skills, attitudes, project experiences and a passion for learning, not just grades. 

Furthermore, the location of the company is very close to my home, and this is very convenient for my internship in the future and makes me even more excited to have this opportunity to join the PPG. I hope that one day I can learn more skills at PPG and contribute my abilities to the team!
