# SECP1513 - Technology and Information System
👩‍🏫 **Lecturer:** Dr. Aryati binti Bakri  
🏫 **Section:** 02  

This course introduces the basic concepts of information systems and technology in organizations. It covers topics such as computer hardware, software, networking and system development.

## 🧠 Reflection
In this course, I learn about hardware, software, databases and network communications. I hope in the future, there will be more hands on lab sessions, just like the earlier PC Assembly lab practical because I found that is a great way for me to learn practical skills and understand more about PC components. Besides that, I hope to have more industry talks as I found that it helped me gain a better understanding of the job roles, skill requirements and future job opportunities which are not covered in textbooks. From my perspective, attending an industry talk will improve our soft skills such as communication, teamwork and collaboration because we are required to do an assignment or report with all of our team members after each talk. a clearer picture of how IT professionals contribute to different industries and inspired me to continue learning about technology’s impact on society.
