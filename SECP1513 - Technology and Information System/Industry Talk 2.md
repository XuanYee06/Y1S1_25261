## 🧠 Reflection
This is my second time attending a industry talk at Hyflex Classroom. Throughout the talk, I understood the important of planning and process development before building a system. One thing I clearly remember from the industry talk was when Ts. Hj. Abdul Alim emphasized that we should start struggling from now while we are still a student. He advised us not to rely too much on AI. Although we can speed up our progress and work faster, we should not overuse it to do everything. Instead, we need to use AI wisely as a supporting tool, not as a shortcut to replace our mindset and own thinking.

Moreover, the explanation of project management methodologies such as Waterfall and Agile also helped me to understand the importance of planning in system development. Thus, we should choose the correct method based on different projects.

All in all, this industry talk motivated me to start cultivate good habits now because struggling now is better than struggling later in the industry. Since understanding is more important than memorizing, I should always plan, learn continuously and understand how systems work in real situations.
