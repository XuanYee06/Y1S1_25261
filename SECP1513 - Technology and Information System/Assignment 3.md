## 🧠 Reflection
This is my second time attending an industry talk at Hyflex Classroom. Throughout the talk, I understood the importance of planning before building a system and project implementation. One thing that I still remember from the industry talk was when Ts. Hj. Abdul Alim emphasized that we should start struggling from now while we are still a student. He advised us not to depend so much on AI. Although we can speed up our progress and work faster, we must not overuse it for doing everything. Instead, we have to use AI wisely as a tool to support us, but not as a shortcut to replace our mindsets and own thinking.

Moreover, the explanation of methodologies such as Waterfall and Agile in project management helped me to understand the importance of planning in system development. Thus, we need to choose the correct methodology for different projects.

All in all, this industry talk really motivated me to start cultivating good habits now because it is better to struggle now than to struggle later in the industry. Since understanding is more important than just memorizing, I hmust always plan, learn continuously and understand how systems work in real situations.
